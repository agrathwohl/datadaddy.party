Who be ye, Datadaddy?

Datadaddy is the hostname I was able to assign my FreeBSD storage server that was available on the .party TLD.

I’m a music composer - a dreadfully romantic one who, through his hybrid of various obsessions, came to understand music very differently from most. Through various happenstances, I have landed upon a few monumental discoveries related to music, sonic perception, and human learning, which fall on largely deaf ears. Our ears inhabit a world sadly declining in its ability to comprehend the messages fed to its most finely-tuned sensory organ, and I am doing what I can to reverse this horrific trend.

Of course, this is no career for me - rather, it’s a life passion. The career I leverage mainly to facilitate my passionate exploration of my musical ideology and its associated tennants, namely:

  • Exploring solutions to musical education, which in turn solves a lot of other education-related problems (these being the primary thing in the way of my ability to proliferate my musical message)
  • Fighting corporatist intellectual property law
  • Bypassing fascistic censorship and other forms of digital oppression - indeed, even when it means defending hateful speech

Why is Datadaddy partying?

Simply put, I’m partying because there’s never been a sweeter time to hoarde digital data than right now. 40TB of high-quality art and counting!
